Service 03

Mulch & Bed Care

Fresh mulch doesn't just look great — it insulates plant roots, retains moisture, suppresses weeds, and gives your landscaping a polished, finished appearance. We handle full mulch installation and ongoing bed maintenance.

Our bed care service includes weeding, edging bed borders, trimming back overgrown plants, and installing fresh mulch to the proper depth. We use high-quality hardwood or dyed mulch in the color of your choice.

What's Included

  • Remove existing weeds from all beds
  • Edge and define bed borders
  • Install fresh mulch (2–3 inch depth)
  • Trim back overgrown shrubs or plants
  • Cleanup and debris removal
  • Choice of mulch color/type

Service 04

Fall & Spring Cleanup

Seasonal cleanups are essential for a healthy lawn year-round. Our fall cleanup prepares your property for winter, and our spring cleanup gets everything ready for the growing season — so your lawn comes back strong.

These are comprehensive services that go far beyond a regular mow visit. We typically spend several hours on a property, ensuring everything is thoroughly cleared, trimmed, and prepped before we leave.

Fall Cleanup Includes

  • Complete leaf removal from lawn & beds
  • Blow out leaves from corners & edges
  • Cut back perennials and ornamental grasses
  • Final lawn mowing & edging
  • Bag and haul away all debris

Spring Cleanup Includes

  • Remove winter debris & dead material
  • Rake and dethatch lawn
  • Edge all beds and define borders
  • First mowing of the season
  • Apply pre-emergent if requested

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with GreenCut.

How do I get a quote?

Getting a quote is easy and free. Fill out our contact form, give us a call, or send us an email with your property address. We'll get back to you within one business day with a customized price.

Do I need to be home during service?

Nope! As long as we have access to your lawn, you don't need to be home. We'll complete the service and send you a notification when we're done. Just make sure gates are unlocked on your service day.

What if it rains on my service day?

We monitor weather closely and will reschedule your service if conditions aren't safe for mowing. We'll always notify you of any changes and work to get you rescheduled as quickly as possible.

Do you offer discounts for weekly service?

Yes! Clients who commit to weekly service receive a 10% discount compared to our bi-weekly rate. Seasonal contract holders also receive priority scheduling and discounted rates on add-on services.

Are you insured?

Absolutely. GreenCut is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry general liability insurance and workers' compensation for your complete peace of mind. Certificates of insurance are available upon request.

How do I pay for services?

We accept cash, check, credit/debit card, and electronic bank transfer. Invoices are sent via email after each service. Monthly billing plans are also available for recurring customers.

Do I need to sign a contract?

No long-term contracts required for our regular mowing service. You can pause or cancel at any time with 48 hours notice. Snow removal seasonal contracts are available for guaranteed winter coverage.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Maplewood, Cedarville, Brookfield, Riverside Heights, Pinecrest, Oak Park, Elmhurst, Fernwood, Laurel Grove, Stonegate, Willow Run, and Greenfield. Not sure? Contact us and we'll let you know!
