Common Questions

FAQ — Before You Reach Out

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, we recommend booking 12–18 months in advance for peak season dates (May–October). Corporate events and parties can often be booked 3–6 months out.

Do you have a minimum budget requirement?

Our day-of coordination packages start at $1,200. Full planning packages start at $6,500. We're happy to discuss what's realistic for your budget during your consultation.

Do you work outside the local area?

Yes — we plan destination events and have worked across the country. Travel fees apply for events more than 50 miles from our office.

Can I just hire you for one part of the planning?

Absolutely. Our partial planning and à la carte services are designed exactly for clients who want help with specific aspects — vendor sourcing, design, timeline, etc.

How many events do you take on at once?

We deliberately limit our client load so every event gets the attention it deserves. We typically work with no more than 2 weddings per weekend.

What if something goes wrong on the day?

That's exactly what we're there for. We anticipate issues before they happen and resolve any surprises calmly and invisibly so your day stays perfect.
