Before Your Visit

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to my appointment?

For massage therapy, you'll undress to your comfort level and be draped with a sheet at all times — only the area being worked is uncovered. For acupuncture, loose, comfortable clothing that rolls up past the knees and elbows is ideal.

Is acupuncture painful?

Most clients report minimal to no pain from acupuncture needles. You may feel a slight pinch, a mild ache, or a tingling sensation — often described as a "de qi" sensation that signals the treatment is working. Many clients fall asleep during sessions.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies by individual and condition. For acute issues, 3–6 sessions often provide significant relief. Chronic conditions typically benefit from 8–12 sessions over 2–3 months. We'll give you an honest assessment at your initial consultation.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations (under 24 hours) or no-shows may incur a $35 fee. We understand emergencies happen — please call us as soon as possible.

Can I receive massage if I'm pregnant?

Yes — we offer specialized prenatal massage for clients in their second and third trimesters. Please inform us at booking so your therapist can prepare appropriate positioning and avoid contraindicated pressure points.

Do you accept insurance?

We are currently out-of-network for most insurance plans. However, we provide detailed receipts/superbills that many clients successfully submit for HSA/FSA reimbursement or out-of-network benefits. Check with your insurance provider.
