Lead Practitioner

Dr. Maya Chen, L.Ac., LMT

Licensed Acupuncturist & Massage Therapist

Maya's path to healing work began with her own experience of burnout and chronic pain in her late twenties. After years of seeking help through conventional medicine with limited results, she discovered acupuncture — and within months, her life had changed. She knew immediately that she wanted to offer that same transformation to others.

She completed her Master of Science in Traditional Chinese Medicine at the Oregon College of Oriental Medicine, graduating with honors in 2012. She is board-certified by the National Certification Commission for Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine (NCCAOM) and licensed in the state of Oregon.

In addition to her acupuncture training, Maya holds a 1,000-hour massage therapy certification and has completed advanced training in prenatal massage, oncology massage, and lymphatic drainage. She brings a truly integrative approach to every client session.

Our Approach

Philosophy of Care

Three pillars that guide every treatment decision we make.

👂

Listen First

Before we touch you, we listen. Every session begins with a genuine conversation about where you are, what's brought you in, and what success looks like for you. Your story shapes your treatment.

🌱

Treat the Root

We don't just chase symptoms. We look at the whole picture — sleep, stress, nutrition, movement, and emotional wellbeing — to understand and address the underlying cause of your condition.

🤝

Partner in Healing

We see ourselves as a partner in your health journey, not the sole solution. We'll recommend complementary lifestyle practices, refer to other specialists when appropriate, and cheer every milestone.
