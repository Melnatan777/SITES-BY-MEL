My Approach

Editing Style & Philosophy

1

Clean, Timeless Edits

No heavy filters, trendy presets, or over-processed looks that date a photo. My editing is designed to look good in 2025 and still hold up in 2050.

2

True-to-Life Color

Skin tones that look like skin. Skies that look like sky. I work hard to preserve the actual colors of your day, not impose a fake mood over them.

3

Documentary First

I direct when needed, but I prefer to observe. The best shots are almost always the ones nobody planned — and you need a photographer paying attention to catch them.

4

Light Over Everything

Every location choice, timing decision, and camera setting comes back to light. Good light makes ordinary moments extraordinary.

5

Low Volume, High Care

I limit my bookings intentionally. Every client gets genuine attention, not a rushed shoot from a photographer on their 4th session of the day.

The Process

What to Expect at Your Session

1

Initial Inquiry

You reach out and tell me about your vision. I'll respond within 24 hours to confirm availability and discuss details.
