Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book through our contact form, call us directly, or use our online booking link. We recommend booking at least 3–5 days in advance, especially on weekends and during holiday periods.

What vaccinations are required?

All pets must be current on rabies and DHPP (for dogs) or FVRCP (for cats). Bordetella (kennel cough) is strongly recommended for boarding and daycare clients. Please bring proof of vaccinations on your first visit.

My dog is anxious — can you still groom them?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience with nervous and anxious pets. We use calm handling techniques and take breaks as needed. Please let us know ahead of time if your pet has special needs so we can plan accordingly.

How long does grooming take?

Grooming times vary by breed, coat type, and size. Small dogs typically take 1.5–2 hours, medium dogs 2–3 hours, and large dogs 3–4 hours. We'll give you an estimated pick-up window when you drop off.

Do you groom cats?

Yes! We offer full cat grooming services including bath, blow-dry, brush-out, nail trim, and coat trim if needed. Cat grooming starts at $55. Please call us to book a cat grooming appointment.

What's your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Same-day cancellations may be subject to a $15 fee. We understand emergencies happen and will always try to work with you.
