Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your appointment

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes — we recommend booking at least a few days ahead for most services. For color services, lash extensions, and packages, we suggest booking 1–2 weeks ahead as those slots fill quickly.

What's your cancellation policy?

We ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service price.

Are your stylists licensed?

Yes — every technician at Glow Studio holds a current state cosmetology or esthetics license and participates in ongoing education to stay current with trends and techniques.

Do you offer consultations?

We offer complimentary consultations for color corrections, balayage, lash extensions, and any service that requires planning. Just give us a call or book a consultation slot online.

What if I'm not happy with my service?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're unhappy with any service, contact us within 5 days and we'll do everything we can to make it right at no additional charge.

Can I bring a child to my appointment?

To maintain a relaxing environment for all guests, we ask that you make childcare arrangements before your appointment. Children receiving services are always welcome!
