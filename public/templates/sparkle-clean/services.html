Deep Clean

Our deep clean is a comprehensive top-to-bottom cleaning that covers everything in our standard service plus all the areas that get overlooked during regular cleanings — baseboards, inside appliances, window tracks, light fixtures, and more.

Recommended for first-time clients, before or after seasonal events, or any time your home needs a full reset. Allow 4–6 hours for a thorough deep clean.

Everything in Standard Clean, Plus:

  • Inside oven, refrigerator & dishwasher
  • Baseboards wiped throughout home
  • Window sills and tracks cleaned
  • Light switches and door handles sanitized
  • Cabinet fronts and drawer pulls wiped
  • Ceiling fans and light fixtures dusted
  • Behind and under furniture (if accessible)

Move-In / Move-Out Clean

Moving is stressful enough. Let us handle the cleaning so you can focus on everything else. Our move-in/out clean is our most comprehensive service — we leave the property spotless so you can get your deposit back or start fresh in a new home.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be home during the clean?

No — most of our clients leave a key or provide a door code. We're fully background-checked and insured, so you can feel confident letting us in. We'll notify you when we arrive and when we're done.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes! We bring all our own professional-grade, eco-friendly cleaning products and equipment. If you have a preferred product or a sensitivity to certain ingredients, just let us know and we'll accommodate.

What's your satisfaction guarantee?

If you're not 100% satisfied with your clean, call us within 24 hours and we'll come back and re-clean the area in question at no additional charge. Your satisfaction is our priority.

How do I pay?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, Venmo, Zelle, and cash. Payment is due on the day of service. Recurring clients can set up automatic billing for hassle-free payment.

Are your cleaners background-checked?

Absolutely. Every member of our cleaning team is fully background-checked before they're hired, and we carry full liability insurance and workers' compensation. Your home and family are protected.

Can I skip or reschedule a visit?

Yes — we just ask for 48 hours notice for rescheduling or cancellations. We'll always do our best to accommodate your schedule changes and find a time that works for you.
