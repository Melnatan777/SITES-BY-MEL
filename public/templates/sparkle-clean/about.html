Green Cleaning

Our Eco-Friendly Commitment

From day one, Sparkle Clean Co. has been committed to using only eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products. We believe a clean home should be healthy — for your family, your pets, and the planet.

We don't use harsh bleaches or chemical sprays that leave residue behind. Our professional-grade green products are just as effective (often more so) than conventional cleaners, and they're safe for children and pets to be around after we leave.

We also minimize our environmental footprint by using reusable microfiber cloths, refillable product dispensers, and efficient routing to reduce our carbon emissions.
