About Me

Sarah Mitchell

Licensed Realtor — Texas License #TX-123456

I grew up in Houston, and there’s no city I know better — the neighborhoods, the school districts, the pockets of value, and the hidden gems that never make it to a national listing site. That local knowledge is your edge when you work with me.

I got my license in 2012, right after the market recovered, and I’ve navigated every kind of market since: competitive bidding wars, slow inventory winters, rising rate environments, and everything in between. I know how to position my clients to win — whether they’re buying or selling.

My clients describe me as calm, honest, and relentlessly available. I don’t disappear after a contract is signed. I answer texts on weekends. I’ve been to closing tables on Christmas Eve. This is my career and my calling, and I take both seriously.
