About DetailPro

Over a decade of passion, precision, and professional results.

Our Story

Built on Passion

DetailPro started in a two-car garage with one rule: never let a car leave unless it looks better than the day it was bought. What started as a weekend side hustle quickly became a full-time passion business.

We've detailed everything from beat-up beaters to six-figure supercars. The attention and care we give every vehicle is exactly the same — because every customer deserves the best.

Today, DetailPro is a trusted name in the community with over 3,100 happy customers and a team of certified detailing professionals who share the same obsession with quality.
