Welcome

A Place to Belong

Cornerstone Fellowship Church has been serving the Houston community since 1998. We believe that every person is known and loved by God — and we’re here to help people discover that truth for themselves.

Whether you’re returning to faith, exploring for the first time, or looking for a spiritual home, we want to walk that road with you.

“Come to me, all who are weary and burdened, and I will give you rest.”
