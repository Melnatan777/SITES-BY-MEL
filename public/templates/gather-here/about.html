Our History

25 Years of Grace

Cornerstone Fellowship Church was founded in 1998 by Pastor James and Linda Carter, who began meeting with twelve families in a living room in the Heights neighborhood of Houston. Their vision was simple: a church where Scripture was central, community was genuine, and every person felt known.

Over the next two decades, that vision grew into a congregation of over 800 members spanning every generation, background, and walk of life. We moved into our current building in 2006, opened a second service in 2011, and launched our Spanish-language ministry in 2018.

Milestones

Our Journey

A church shaped by faithfulness across the decades.

1998

Founded in a Living Room

Pastor James and Linda Carter begin meeting with 12 families for Bible study, worship, and prayer in the Heights area.

2001

First Building

The congregation grows to 120 members and moves into a rented storefront space, launching its first youth ministry.

2006

Our Current Home

Cornerstone dedicates its current 500-seat sanctuary at 1234 Elm Street, debt-free thanks to generous members.

2011

Second Sunday Service Added

Continued growth calls for a second service. The church now serves over 500 weekly attendees.

2018

Spanish-Language Ministry

A Friday night Spanish-language group launches, reflecting Houston’s beautiful diversity and the church’s commitment to welcome all.

2023

25th Anniversary

Cornerstone celebrates 25 years with a baptism service, community festival, and the launch of a new outreach food pantry serving 200 families monthly.
