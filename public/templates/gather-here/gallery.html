New Life in Christ

Baptism Celebrations

Each baptism is one of our most joyful moments as a church. When someone publicly declares their faith through baptism, the whole congregation celebrates together.

We hold baptism services multiple times a year. If you or someone you love is ready to take this step, we’d love to walk through it together.

“We were therefore buried with him through baptism into death in order that, just as Christ was raised from the dead through the glory of the Father, we too may live a new life.”
Romans 6:4
