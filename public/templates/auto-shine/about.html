Meet the Owner

Hi, I'm [Your Name]

I've been obsessed with cars since I could hold a wash mitt. What started as detailing neighbor's cars on weekends turned into a full-time business built on one simple promise: treat every vehicle like it's mine.

After years of working in professional auto body shops and completing my IDA certification, I launched AutoShine with the belief that every car owner deserves pro-level results — and no one should have to waste a Saturday sitting in a waiting room to get them.

Today, our team of certified detailers has transformed over 2,400 vehicles. We use only the highest-quality products, maintain our equipment rigorously, and never cut corners — even when the customer can't see it.

When I'm not detailing cars, you'll find me at car shows, testing new ceramic coating technology, or teaching weekend workshops on paint care. This is our craft, and we take it seriously.

Our Journey

How AutoShine Grew

From a single detail kit to a fully equipped mobile operation — here's how we got here.

2018

Where It All Started

First detail kit purchased. First paying customer: a neighbor's filthy minivan. They were blown away. Word spread fast.

2019

IDA Certification Earned

Completed the International Detailing Association certification program, formalizing skills built over years of hands-on work.

2020

First Mobile Rig Launched

Invested in a fully equipped trailer with water tanks, generator, and pro-grade tools. True mobile detailing, anywhere.

2022

Ceramic Coating Certified

Became a Gtechniq-certified installer, opening ceramic coating as a premium service offering.

2024

2,000+ Cars Milestone

Hit the milestone of 2,000 vehicles detailed — and still counting. Added a second technician to meet demand.
