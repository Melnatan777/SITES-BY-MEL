Common Questions

Before You Come In

Is the assessment really free?

100% free, zero strings. We do assessments because it helps us recommend the right program — and because it gives you a chance to meet your coach and see the gym before committing to anything.

What should I wear and bring?

Athletic clothing you can move in and athletic shoes. We'll do some light movement screening, so comfort matters. Bring a water bottle — we have filtered water on site.

I'm completely out of shape. Is this for me?

Especially for you. We work with true beginners every single day. Everyone starts somewhere — and walking in the door is always the hardest step. We'll meet you exactly where you are.

Do you work with clients who have injuries?

Yes — with physician's clearance when needed. Our coaches are trained in corrective exercise and we always work around limitations, not through them. Share everything on your form and we'll plan accordingly.

Can I try a group class before joining?

Absolutely. We offer one complimentary class to new visitors so you can experience the energy and community before committing. Just mention it when you contact us.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients notice energy improvements and feel stronger within 2–3 weeks. Visible body composition changes typically begin around 6–8 weeks with consistent training and nutrition habits in place.
