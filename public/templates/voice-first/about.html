Hi, I’m Alex

Writer, podcaster, and recovering perfectionist.

I've spent the last decade trying to figure out how to do creative work sustainably — how to keep making things you care about without burning out, losing your voice, or selling out along the way. Everything I publish is my best attempt to share what I've learned.

I started writing on the internet in 2015, mostly for an audience of twelve people (hi, Mom). I launched my newsletter in 2020 during the pandemic because I needed somewhere to think out loud, and somehow 8,400 people decided they wanted to read my thoughts every Thursday. Still surprised by that.

The podcast came next — 142 episodes and counting of honest conversations with writers, podcasters, photographers, educators, and entrepreneurs about the real work behind creative careers. Not the highlight reel. The actual struggle, the weird pivots, the late nights, and the moments that made it all worth it.

How I Got Here

The Journey

A winding path from corporate marketing to full-time creator — and all the lessons in between.

2015

First Blog Post

Published my first piece of writing on the internet: a 400-word post about why I quit my agency job to freelance. 12 people read it. I was terrified and exhilarated.

2017

Going Full-Time Freelance

Left the last of my corporate clients and committed fully to building my own audience. Scariest and best decision I've ever made. Ate a lot of rice and beans that year.

2019

First Speaking Gig

Was invited to speak at a small content marketing conference. 80 people in the audience. I rehearsed 47 times. Got a standing ovation. Cried in the bathroom after.

2020

Newsletter Launch

Launched The Weekly Letter during pandemic lockdowns. In the first month: 200 subscribers. By month 6: 2,000. By year's end: 5,000. The internet is a wild place.

2021

The Podcast Begins

Launched The Thoughtful Creator Podcast with zero audience, borrowed equipment, and a closet full of coats for soundproofing. Episode 1 got 87 downloads. Episode 50 got 12,000.

Today

Still Figuring It Out

8,400 newsletter subscribers, 142 podcast episodes, 24K Instagram followers, and still the same question every Thursday morning: "Does this matter? Will anyone care?" Shipping it anyway.

What Drives Me

My Values

Radical Honesty
