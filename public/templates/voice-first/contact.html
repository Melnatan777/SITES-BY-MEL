Before You Reach Out

Common Questions

Do you speak at virtual events?

Yes — about 40% of my speaking is virtual. Virtual keynote rate is $1,500, and I bring the same energy and preparation as an in-person appearance.

What brands do you NOT work with?

I don't partner with brands in tobacco, gambling, MLMs, or any product I wouldn't genuinely recommend to a close friend. Authenticity isn't just a word — it's the reason my audience trusts me.

Can I pitch myself as a podcast guest?

Yes! Fill out the form with "Podcast Guest" as your inquiry type. Please share who you are, what you'd talk about, and why it serves my audience. Unsolicited bulk pitches are ignored.

Do you offer free strategy calls?

I offer a free 20-minute discovery call for potential consulting clients and brand partners. Book one above. General advice calls are paid — my newsletter is free and covers most questions!

How quickly do you respond?

Within 48 business hours, always. If you haven't heard from me in 3 business days, please follow up — sometimes things get buried and a gentle nudge is totally appropriate.

Do you write guest posts or contribute to publications?

Occasionally, for the right fit. I prioritize my own newsletter and podcast, but I'm open to contributing to outlets that genuinely serve thoughtful creators. Pitch via the form.
