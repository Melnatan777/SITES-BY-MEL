Our Story

A Shop Built on Intention

Blush & Co. started as a small market booth in 2019 with a table of handmade earrings and a dream. Today we curate goods from over 30 independent makers and artists — all women-owned businesses.

Every product in our shop is something we'd give to a friend. That standard hasn't changed since day one.

“I didn't want to just sell things. I wanted to create a space where small makers could shine and customers could find something truly special.”

— Jamie, Founder
